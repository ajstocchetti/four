All Numbers Lead To 4

Can you figure out how?





To play, simply enter a number in the input box and click 'Show Path' to see how your number gets to four. Once you think you've figured it out, enter a number and click 'Show Path & Solution' to see if you are correct.

While technically this works for all numbers, let's only play with whole numbers. Numbers less than zero, decimals, fractions, imaginary numbers, and other mathematical oddities add unnecessary complexity. So let's just stick to whole numbers for now. Thanks!
